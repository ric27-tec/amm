Virtual Sets & Webshows

We create custom webshows with 2D or 3D virtual sets designed around your brand, message and audience.

Why produce your webshow with us?

Brands, institutions and professionals increasingly need their own audiovisual space. A virtual set allows you to present your message with consistency, flexibility and a much stronger visual identity, whether you need a corporate webinar, a branded webshow, a launch or an educational series.

What we offer

  • Studios equipped with advanced technology including AI tools, virtual environments and 2D/3D graphics.
  • Professional lighting adaptable to your brand style and production goals.
  • High-quality microphones and noise reduction workflows for clean audio.
  • Physical and virtual camera setups for a dynamic and modern look.
  • Recording and simultaneous streaming to YouTube, TikTok, LinkedIn, Facebook and Instagram.
  • A catalog of customizable 2D and 3D virtual environments compatible with presentations and branded assets.
  • Audience interaction, live calls, giveaways and other engaging on-screen elements.
