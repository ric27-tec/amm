We have delivered more than 300 audiovisual projects. Many are private or restricted-access, so here is a curated selection of public work that reflects our quality and range.

Audiovisual production for FINANCOOP’s 26th anniversary

Full production for a hybrid event with high visual impact and smooth coordination between on-site and remote speakers.

  • 12 m LED wall split into 3 independent sections
  • Closed-circuit multicamera setup plus live streaming
  • Stage design with sofas, interactive lectern and speaker timers
  • DMX lighting show and professional audio
  • A dynamic experience with strong visual execution and better audience connection

Hybrid event streaming - “Orígenes” by DINERS CLUB

Technical operation and live streaming designed to deliver a stable signal and a strong audiovisual experience for a wide audience.

  • Closed-circuit cameras and presentation management
  • Signal mixing for LED screens and streaming
  • Integration of microphone audio with the venue’s technical team
  • Full recording for future distribution
  • Reliable streaming with a smooth viewer experience

Hybrid event streaming - “TODOS TRANSITAMOS” by CARE

Live streaming production with music integration, focused on accessibility, technical clarity and audio quality.

  • Presenter audio and closed-circuit camera coverage
  • Live mix and multiplatform delivery
  • Integration of microphones and live instruments
  • Full recording for redistribution
  • An inclusive audiovisual experience with strong live-event energy

Webshow production - “Estoy Aquí”

Production of 18 studio episodes with live questions, polls and social media integration for national public communication.

  • Webshow built on a 4-camera system
  • Live switching and delivery to YouTube, Facebook Live and Instagram
  • Integration of audiovisual assets and live audience questions
  • Full recording for redistribution
  • Dynamic format with strong visual value and audience engagement

Audiovisual production - “Interhospitalaria” scientific meeting

Hybrid production for the Ecuadorian Society of Dermatology with technical support for both in-person and remote participants.

  • Closed-circuit cameras plus live streaming
  • Integrated room audio with a dedicated stream mix
  • Management of PPTs and other AV resources
  • A polished and reliable production workflow for the medical field

Recording and post-production - “Espacio Inspira”

Recording and editing of an interview with Sebastián “El Zuko” Carrasco, optimized for digital distribution across platforms.

  • Pre-recorded webshow with a 3-camera system
  • Multicamera capture with pro sound and studio lighting
  • Editing with AV inserts and color correction
  • Multiple deliverables for social media and digital platforms
  • Fast and polished production tailored to digital content
